Privacy Policy for Azkar

Last updated: 06 March 2026

1. About Azkar

Azkar is a simple dua and dhikr application designed for people of all age groups. It helps users read and recite duas and adhkar in a clean, distraction‑free interface.

2. No data collection

Azkar does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data or usage data about you.

In particular, Azkar does not:

  • Require you to create an account or sign in.
  • Collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details.
  • Collect location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics.
  • Use third‑party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

All dua and dhikr content is provided directly in the app and can be used offline. Any preferences saved (such as theme or language) are stored only on your device and are not accessible to us.

3. Children’s privacy

Azkar is suitable for all age groups, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, we also do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

4. Third‑party services

Azkar does not display ads and does not integrate any third‑party services that collect user data. If a future version of the app ever includes links to external websites (for example, to open a support email or documentation), those websites will have their own privacy policies.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if store rules change. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Azkar after any change means you accept the updated policy.
